10 concurrent agents each record 20 corroborations to ONE shared row (expected total 200):

Naive read-modify-write:           final=31    expected=200  lost_updates=169  (2.0s)
CockroachDB SERIALIZABLE:          final=200   expected=200  lost_updates=0    (8.9s)

Naive mode silently LOST 169 updates to race conditions.
SERIALIZABLE isolation lost ZERO - every concurrent write is
preserved. The memory is a transactional ledger of truths,
not just an index. This is the CockroachDB difference.
